NexTask: The AI-First Marketplace Strategy

1. Vision & The "Peace" Philosophy

NexTask was founded as a direct response to the "chaos" of traditional freelance platforms like Upwork. We believe that when freelancers are at peace, clients receive the highest quality work. Our mission is to dismantle the "race to the bottom" and replace it with a Transparency-First model.

The Conflict of 2026: The Efficiency Arbitrage

Traditional platforms suffer from an information gap. A freelancer uses AI to complete a $\$2,000$ task in 1 hour but bills for 40 hours. This creates a foundation of dishonesty.
NexTask solves this by making AI usage a feature, not a secret.

2. Core Economic Model

The $100 Minimum Floor

To ensure professional standards and prevent "micro-task noise," NexTask enforces a mandatory project floor:

Minimum Fixed-Price Contract: $\$100$

Rationale: A $\$100$ minimum ensures that the freelancer has the budget to perform a professional security audit and architectural review, regardless of how fast the AI generates the code.

The $200/Hour NexTask Referee

For clients "fed up" with Upwork's lack of vetting, we provide the Referee Service:

The Role: Salaried NexTask Employees (Senior Architects).

The Rule: Referees are strictly forbidden from bidding on projects.

The Service: Forensic audits, technical scoping, and milestone verification.

Pricing: $\$200$ per hour for high-level consultancy.
